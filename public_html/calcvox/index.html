About us

Here at CalcVox, we want to even the playing field for blind students in mathematical subjects. To do this, we're building a brand new talking calculator, unlike anything the blind community has ever seen before.

Key features

  • Uses text-to-speech, making sure there's no string or number that the user can't read, and also making the output way less choppy.
  • Built with extensebility in mind, allowing you to replace any of the components at will.
  • distinct feeling buttons, allowing the user to just do math, not have to memorize the buttons.
  • Runs entirely open source, custom built software.
  • Plans to support high-level math concepts (up to and including graphing) entirely through 3D audio.
  • And much, much more.
